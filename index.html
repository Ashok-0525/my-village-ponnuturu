About My Village

My village is located in Kotturu mandal, Srikaulam dist, Andhra pradesh. It is known for its greenery, friendly people, and traditional lifestyle. Most of the villagers are involved in farming and small-scale businesses.

Culture and Festivals

We celebrate all traditional festivals with great joy. Our village has a unique way of celebrating harvest festivals, with folk dances and community feasts.
